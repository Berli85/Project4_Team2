Welcome Amateur Sommeliers!

Wine is one of the most consumed beverages around the world and it has been around since 6000 BCE! It is also a great way to resurface conversations, friendships, meals, late evenings, vacations, dancing, people, and emotions. Therefore, we wanted to learn more about this fascinating beverage by exploring and analyzing different datasets.

We want to encourage the average wine drinker to explore what makes wine… well, wine! What features make a wine fall into one of eight categories? This website breaks down the most looked at feature of wine and how to determine the best quality based on its features, origin, and cost. There are eight different categories of wine: red, white, rosé, orange, sparkling, fortified, ice, and dessert.
